# Mission: Competitive Programming — ICPC Asia Pacific (Thailand) Regional

## Why
The ICPC regional round is in ~10 days (around 2026-07-18). The goal is to walk in able to reliably solve the 2–3 easiest problems of a regional set fast and bug-free, and have a real shot at a 4th.

## Success looks like
- Read a problem's constraints and immediately know which complexity class (and therefore which technique) is required
- Implement sorting/binary-search/two-pointer/greedy/prefix-sum solutions in under 20 minutes each, first-submit-accepted
- Solve basic DP (coin change, grid paths, knapsack) and basic graph problems (BFS/DFS, components, Dijkstra, DSU) without references
- Complete a 5-hour virtual contest solving ≥3 problems before contest day

## Constraints
- 10 days total: 6 full-time days, 4 days at 4–6 hours (user decides which as the schedule unfolds)
- Solo preparation (team trains separately or independently)
- Language: C++ (all existing solutions are C++)
- Current level: reliably solves Codeforces 800-rated problems, some 1000; ~27 problems solved

## Out of scope
- Advanced topics that won't pay off in 10 days: segment trees with lazy propagation, flows, FFT, suffix structures, heavy geometry
- Rating grinding on Codeforces — the target is the ICPC set profile, not ladder climbing
