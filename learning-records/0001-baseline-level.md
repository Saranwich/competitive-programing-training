# Baseline: rusty CF-1000 beginner, end of first-year CS

Self-reported (2026-07-08): CS student who just finished first year. Codeforces rating ~1000. Beginner at CP. Has seen basics like binary search — but **~4 months ago, and is rusty**. Repo git history (~27 solved, Jan 2026) matches: mostly 800-rated A problems, a few Bs, one C.

**Implications:**
- Treat the first ~2 days as *reactivation*, not new material. Binary search etc. are "known but cold" — re-derive them via practice, don't assume retention.
- Rating 1000 (not 800) means the ceiling is real: basic DP + BFS/DFS/Dijkstra/DSU is achievable in 10 days.
- Still: start at CPH ch 2 (complexity), skip C++ syntax teaching.
- Watch for illusion-of-fluency: things he "knows" from 4 months ago need retrieval practice to actually fire under contest pressure. Confirm with quiz scores / timed solves, not self-report.
- Out of scope unchanged: segment trees, flows, FFT, geometry.
