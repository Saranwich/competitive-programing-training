The 10-Day Plan

2026-07-08 → contest (~2026-07-18). 6 full-time days, 4 partial days — you assign which is which each morning.

Daily shape. Full-time day: 2 lessons + ~6 problems + 30 min review of yesterday's misses. Partial day (4–6h): 1 lesson + 3–4 problems. Every problem you fail, you upsolve the same day — that's where the learning is.

Rules of the 10 days

  • Typing beats reading. Cap book reading at 45 min per topic; the rest is solving.
  • 20-minute rule. Stuck 20 min with no idea → read the editorial, then implement it yourself without looking back.
  • Log every miss. One line per failed problem: what category of mistake (wrong idea / wrong complexity / bug). Day 10 reviews this log.
  • Out of scope stays out. No segment trees, flows, FFT. See MISSION.md.
